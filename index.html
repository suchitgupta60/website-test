Who We Are

ONE STOP FOR EVERYTHING
IMPACTFUL CAMPAIGNS ACROSS MULTIPLE PLATFORMS

Ockupy is a full-service digital marketing and business services firm that helps brands not only launch impactful campaigns across multiple platforms, but also provides the infrastructure to build and run a successful business. We provide a wide range of tools to help brands develop and curate their message and content, market and sell their product, and scale their business – all in one place.

SOCIAL MEDIA & INFLUENCER MARKETING

BRAND DEVELOPMENT & MANAGEMENT

Ockupy lets brands leverage our influencer network across multiple platforms; we find influencers who can authentically tell a story so that brands can effectively attract, convert, and engage consumers using the power of social influence. We manage these influencers and campaigns so you don't have to.

We can provide monthly management of your business's social media accounts – Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, branded blogs, etc – and develop unique and consistent marketing messages for your brand. If you need help fine-tuning your brand's look, feel, and message, we are pros at that too.

INCENTIVE

AD MARKETING

Did you know it costs a business between 5-10x more to acquire a new customer than it does to sell to an existing one? This is where incentive and loyalty marketing come in, and we can develop ways to keep your customers coming back, whether it's a points reward system, an upfront VIP cost (think Amazon Prime), or all-inclusive offers.

Everyone likes free items. We provoke brand engagement using campaigns that offer prizes and rewards that encourage people to buy or sell through our creative techniques.
